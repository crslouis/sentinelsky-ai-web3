## Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks

**Risk**: MiMo API downtime or rate limits
**Mitigation**: Caching, fallback models, graceful degradation

**Risk**: False positives harm reputation
**Mitigation**: Human review, confidence scores, user feedback

**Risk**: Scalability issues at high volume
**Mitigation**: Auto-scaling, caching, optimization

### Business Risks

**Risk**: Low user adoption
**Mitigation**: Free tier, community building, partnerships

**Risk**: Competition from established players
**Mitigation**: Focus on AI differentiation, speed, multilingual

**Risk**: Regulatory challenges
**Mitigation**: Legal compliance, responsible disclosure, transparency

### Market Risks

**Risk**: Crypto market downturn
**Mitigation**: Diversify to enterprise, focus on security (always needed)

**Risk**: Changing threat landscape
**Mitigation**: Continuous learning, community input, rapid iteration
